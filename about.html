Mathematics Corner

Here's one of my favorite mathematical identities, Euler's formula:

\[e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0\]

This beautiful equation connects five fundamental mathematical constants: - \(e\) (Euler's number) - \(i\) (imaginary unit) - \(\pi\) (pi) - \(1\) (multiplicative identity) - \(0\) (additive identity)
